At KOREAN HUB the workshops are dynamic and participatory. Attendees fully prepare recipes with organic and korean products, with the idea of ​​promoting a quality, healthy and sustainable diet. At the end of the workshop, the dishes are shared in an informal and relaxed atmosphere.
